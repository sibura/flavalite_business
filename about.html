About Modern Business

FlavaLite Innovations is a multi-award winning technology innovations company founded in 2011 by Vuyelwa Bam, Matsepo Africa, Sibongiseni Tunzelana, and Unathi Tunzelana with the aim of pioneering innovative products in Information Technology (IT) service delivery.

FlavaLite Innovations is a 100 percent black women owned enterprise. Our vision is to trail-blaze technology innovations with a heart. We are on a mission to serve up world class technology solutions, consciously.
